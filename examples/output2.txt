PROBLEM

A farmer has 17 sheep.
All but 9 die.

FACTS

- Total sheep = 17
- 9 sheep survive

POTENTIAL TRAPS

- Misinterpreting "all but 9"
- Performing unnecessary subtraction

REASONING

All but 9 means all except 9 die.

Therefore, 9 remain alive.

VERIFICATION

17 - 8 = 9 survivors

FINAL ANSWER

9 sheep are left.
