PROBLEM

5 machines make 5 products in 5 minutes.

FACTS

- Each machine makes 1 product in 5 minutes.

POTENTIAL TRAPS

- Multiplying everything together
- Assuming more products require more time

REASONING

1 machine makes 1 product in 5 minutes.

100 machines simultaneously make 100 products.

VERIFICATION

Production rate remains unchanged.

FINAL ANSWER

5 minutes
